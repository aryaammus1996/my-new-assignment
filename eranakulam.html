About Ernakulam


The district includes Kochi, also known as the commercial capital of Kerala,which is famous for its ancient Hindu temples, churches,synagogues and mosques.The district includes the largest metropolitan region of the state .Kochi is regarded as the gateway to Kerala as it is connected all the major cities in India.It is known as the Queen of Arabian sea.
