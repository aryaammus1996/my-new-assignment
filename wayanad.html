WAYANAD

About Wayanad


Wayanad is arural district in Kerala state,southwest India.In the east,the Wayanad wildlife sanctuary is a lush,forested region with areas of high altitude, home to animals including Asiatic elephants ,tigers,leopards and egrets.Wayanad is a part of aforest reserve,located on the border of Tamilnadu and Kerala. Because of it wild green courage it offers great scenic beauty along with the experience of seeing wildlife.



PLACE TO VISIT IN WAYANAD


EDAKKAL CAVES



Edakkal is a remote location in Wayanad district of Kerala.The tallest mountain in the district Ambukuthy mala is located here.Three pre historic caves known as th Edakkal Caves, dating from at least 5000 BC.There are stone age pictorical writings on the walls of these natural caves.

BAMBOO FOREST PARK



Bamboo forest in Wayanad , if you wish to give a boost to your Wayanad vacation with a bit of eco tourism ,then Bamboo forest is one of the most surreal plces that you should not miss out upon.Bamboo forest are protected by government authorities and Known for collectinga grain called "bamboo rice".

BANASURA SAGAR DAM



Banasura sagar dam,which impounds the Karamanthode tributary of the Kabani river.it was the largest earth dam in India and the second largest of its kind in Asia.The dam is made up of massive stacks of stones and boulders.

THOLPETTY WILDLIFE SANCTUARY



Tholpetty wildlife sanctuary, located 20 kms east of mananthavadi in the district of Wayanad. A verity of large wild animals such as Indian bison elephant, deer and tiger are found there. There are also quite a few unusual birds in the sanctuary
